Tea Portfolio

Kenya's Finest Teas

Kenya is renowned for producing some of the world's finest teas. We offer a comprehensive selection of tea grades, sourced through our trusted auction partners and direct relationships with leading estates.

BP1
Broken Pekoe 1

A well-balanced broken leaf grade offering a smooth, full-bodied cup with bright colour and consistent quality.

PF1
Pekoe Fanning 1

High-quality fannings with excellent liquor characteristics, widely used in premium tea blends and tea bags.

PD
Pekoe Dust

Fine particle grade ideal for tea bags and blends, known for its bold flavour, rich colour, and quick brewing.

Fannings
Standard Fannings

Small leaf particles that brew quickly and deliver a strong, brisk cup – ideal for everyday consumption and bulk blends.

We can create custom tea blends and combinations tailored to your market requirements. Whether you need a specific grade, a unique blend, or private label packaging, our team works with you to deliver exactly what you need.

We work closely with auction houses and producers to ensure consistent quality and competitive pricing. Our tea portfolio is available for export in bulk quantities to markets around the world.
