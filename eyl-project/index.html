EYL Coastal Community

A master-planned coastal development featuring cliff-top villas, a restaurant, infinity pool, and beach cabanas with stunning ocean views.

Project Overview

The EYL Coastal Community is a master-planned development designed to create a luxurious coastal living experience. Located along the pristine coastline of EYL, this community offers residents and visitors a unique blend of natural beauty and modern amenities.

The master plan includes cliff-top villas with panoramic ocean views, a main building with lobby and restaurant, an infinity pool overlooking the sea, private beach cabanas, and direct access to the beach. Future phases will include additional apartment blocks and a community mosque.
