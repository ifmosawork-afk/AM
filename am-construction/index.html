AM Construction

Building the Foundations of East Africa's Future

AM Construction is the construction and development division of AM International Group, focused on delivering projects that contribute to the growth, modernization, and prosperity of East Africa.

Our Purpose

Building a Stronger Future

With a strong focus on Kenya and Somalia, we are committed to supporting the region's development through quality construction, infrastructure improvement, commercial projects, and strategic partnerships.

As East Africa continues to experience rapid urbanization and economic growth, the demand for modern infrastructure, housing, commercial developments, and public facilities continues to increase. AM Construction aims to play a meaningful role in meeting these needs while creating lasting value for communities and businesses.

Our Vision

Trusted Partner in Development

To become a trusted construction and development partner helping shape the future of East Africa through quality, innovation, and sustainable building practices.

Our Focus

Kenya – A Leading Economic Hub

East Africa has historic trade and business roots and is now becoming a leading economic hub, continuing to attract significant investment in infrastructure, real estate, tourism, and commercial development.

AM Construction actively explores opportunities that contribute to:

Residential Developments

We support the construction of modern housing projects designed to meet the needs of growing communities throughout East Africa.

Commercial Buildings

From office complexes to retail developments, we aim to create spaces that support business growth and economic activity.

Infrastructure Projects

Infrastructure is the foundation of economic development. We seek opportunities to contribute to projects that improve connectivity, accessibility, and regional growth.

Industrial Developments

Supporting manufacturing, logistics, and trade requires modern facilities designed for efficiency and long-term growth.

Our Focus

Somalia – Rebuilding and Growth

Why East Africa

A Region of Opportunity

East Africa is one of the fastest-growing regions in the world. A young population, expanding cities, increasing trade, and rising investment create significant opportunities for sustainable development.

At AM Construction, we believe today's construction projects will become tomorrow's schools, businesses, homes, hospitals, and economic centres. By investing in quality development today, we help create stronger communities and a more prosperous future.

Sister Company

AM Energy

AM Energy is the renewable energy and infrastructure division of AM International Group, developing large-scale power generation, transmission, and grid infrastructure across the Horn of Africa.

Together, AM Construction and AM Energy are building the physical and energy infrastructure that will power East Africa's future — from foundations to transmission lines.

Solar Parks

Utility-scale solar farms with battery storage across Kenya and Somalia.

Cross-Border Transmission

Connecting Kenya, Somalia, and the Horn of Africa through a unified power grid.

Oil & Gas

Partnering with the Somali and Turkish governments to develop offshore hydrocarbon resources.

Grid Modernization

Smart grids, battery storage, and AI-powered infrastructure for energy reliability.
