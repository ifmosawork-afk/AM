About AM International Group

Building Connections. Driving Trade.
Shaping the Future.

AM International Group is an international business group founded with a vision to contribute to the economic development and prosperity of East Africa.

Our Story

Founded with Purpose

Established by a young team born and raised in Europe with strong roots in East Africa, our company combines global experience with a deep commitment to the region we proudly call home.

Our journey began with a simple belief: that East Africa possesses extraordinary potential and that sustainable development is achieved through trade, investment, infrastructure, and strong regional partnerships. While our experiences have taken us across different countries and cultures, our mission has always remained focused on creating opportunities for the people and communities of East Africa.

Our Heritage

Inspired by History

For centuries, the East African coast stood at the centre of some of the world's most important trade routes. Merchants from the Swahili Coast, Somalia, Arabia, and beyond exchanged spices, tea, textiles, agricultural products, knowledge, and culture across the Indian Ocean.

These historic connections helped build prosperous communities and thriving commercial centres that linked Africa to global markets.

At AM International Group, we are inspired by this legacy. We believe the future of East Africa can be strengthened by reviving the spirit of regional cooperation, international trade, and entrepreneurship that defined our history.

Our goal is not simply to conduct business but to help reconnect markets, strengthen economic ties, and create opportunities that benefit future generations.

Who We Are

A Diversified Business Group

AM International Group is a diversified business group focused on identifying opportunities that support long-term economic growth and development.

We bring together international perspectives, local knowledge, and strategic partnerships to deliver projects that create lasting value. Through responsible business practices and a commitment to excellence, we aim to contribute to stronger economies and more prosperous communities.

Today, we are actively involved in development and construction-related projects in Kenya and Somalia while expanding partnerships throughout East Africa and the Gulf region.

Our Vision

Connecting East Africa to Global Markets

To become a leading international business group connecting East Africa with global markets through trade, investment, infrastructure development, and sustainable enterprise.

Our Mission

Creating Opportunities, Driving Growth

To create opportunities, strengthen regional and international trade, support economic development, and build businesses that contribute to the long-term prosperity of East Africa and its global partners.
